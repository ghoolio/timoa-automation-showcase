## How this maps to AI automation work

AI automation projects often require more than prompting. They require:

1. Understanding the actual business process.
2. Identifying where automation creates value.
3. Connecting systems safely.
4. Designing fallbacks and manual review paths.
5. Handling sensitive data carefully.
6. Creating understandable documentation.
7. Testing with real users.

Timoa demonstrates this product and engineering mindset.
