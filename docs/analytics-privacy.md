# Analytics and Privacy

## Goal

Timoa analytics should help salon owners and product development without collecting unnecessary personal data.

## Allowed analytics data

- Event names
- Salon-scoped counts
- Booking funnel steps
- Payment method type
- Payment status
- Aggregated service demand
- Aggregated time demand
- Dashboard usage events

## Not tracked

- Customer names
- Customer emails
- Customer phone numbers
- Manage tokens
- Raw Stripe payloads
- Raw PayPal payloads
- Payment secrets
- Precise customer location
- Internal notes

## Insights dashboard

The insights dashboard focuses on aggregated metrics:

- bookings
- cancellations
- online paid revenue
- expected on-site value
- top services
- demand by weekday/hour
- payment method mix
